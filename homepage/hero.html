Batman

I'M VENGEANCE

In my opinion, Batman should be the face of DC comics. He's got the best stories, best villains and his character motivations and development is way better than any other DC hero (Yup, Superman sucks). Also, I love that most of his stories are dark and horror-themed. It gives him a uniquenessthat other characters don't have. The life action adaptations have been on point so far, each iteration showing a different side of his character but the new batman movie with Robert Pattinson has to be the best portrayal of the character so far.
